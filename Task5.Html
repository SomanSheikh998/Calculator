Muhammad Ali Jinnah




Important Glimpses of Jinnah's Life

  • 1876: Born in Wazir Mansion
  • 1895: Became a Barrister from Lincoln's Inn Bar-At-Law
  • 1906: Joined Indian National Congress
  • 1913: Joined All-India Muslim League
  • 1916: Successfully built consensus
  • 1919: Resigned from Imperial Legislative Council as protest to Rowlatt Act
  • 1940: Passed the Pakistan Resolution at Manto Park, Lahore
  • 1947: Became the First Governor-General of Pakistan
  • 1948: Passed away on 11 September
